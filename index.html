01

Leadership Structure

Our club operates with dedicated leadership roles including Overall Coordinator & Co-coordinators, Technical Coordinators, Media Head, Design Head, and Planning & Management Head.
02

Project Teams

Specialized project teams work on our major initiatives: Mars Rover for URC by ISRO, Wall Climber robotics, Micromouse competitions, and Project-X for freshman orientation.
03

Workshop & Learning

Regular workshops, seminars, and hands-on sessions covering Arduino programming, sensor integration, autonomous navigation, and advanced robotics concepts for all skill levels.
04

Competition Participation

Active participation in Inter IIT Tech Meet, Universal Robotics Challenge by ISRO, various company hackathons, and other national-level robotics competitions.
